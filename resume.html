Campaign

My interest in technology and the web began like it has for a lot of folks born in the 80's. I was eleven years old when we inherited our first computer, which through happenstance became my own over the years. I was fixated. I was hired as a pre-teen to design and develop an ecommerce site for an ice skating manufacturer in my hometown.
Following earning my degree as a designer and photographer, my work and experimentation has opened doors for me to work on product teams as a product manager, a product designer, and a UI developer. My trust in myself and my love for collaboration have lead me to found software design firms, to sell, and to found-again.
Human-centered design is a given. So, while our technical advantages are greatly outpacing the worlds imagination, equitable design partnership and well-informed creative product thinking is critical for every aspect of business. I am humbled and thankful that my handiwork is used and depended upon by millions. Today, I'm ready to bring my skillsets....
